About Us

Welcome to Piketplace

The developers behind the 2023 Hackathon winner platform Piketplace, Djedje, Filano were able to create an amazing utility application that will allow pioneers to spend their mined Pi Coins on meaningful purchases. This is a significant development for the Pi Network, as it demonstrates the real-world utility of Pi Coins beyond simply being a speculative investment.

Our Basic Features

Unlocking the Power of Pi Cryptocurrency: Unique Advantages and Features

Shoppings

Piketplace shopping offers a seamless, user-friendly experience for selecting and purchasing items within the Pi Network ecosystem

Sellings

The Sellings feature on Piketplace allows sellers to list and showcase products or services, reaching a broad audience and maximizing sales opportunities

Shipping

This feature streamlines e-commerce logistics, offering a user-friendly platform for efficient goods management and smooth shipping by sellers

Rewarding

Piketplace's Rewarding feature adds extra value by using Piket tokens to recognize and appreciate user contributions, fostering a dynamic and engaging environment.
