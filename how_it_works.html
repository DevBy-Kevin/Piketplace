A Seamless Trading Experience

Whether you’re a buyer, seller, or delivery partner, navigating Piketplace is a breeze. Here’s a step-by-step guide to help you make the most out of your Piketplace journey.

  1. 1-Sign Up for Free
    2. Get started by signing up for a free Piketplace account. Joining our community is easy and takes just a few minutes. Simply visit mainnet.piketplace.com and click on « Login » in the menu. No need for an email – your connection is directly secured through Pinetwork’s Auth Security. You can also test the platform’s features by visiting testnet.piketplace.com
  2. 2-Explore the Marketplace
    3. Once you’re logged in, dive into the marketplace to discover a wide range of products and services from various sellers. Piketplace caters to diverse interests, ensuring there’s something for everyone.

Whether you’re looking for handmade crafts, vintage items, or everyday essentials, Piketplace has it all. b. Place Your Order with Pi Cryptocurrency When you find the perfect item, proceed to checkout. Track Your Order Stay informed about your purchase with real-time order tracking. Know exactly when your item is shipped and when it’s expected to arrive.
